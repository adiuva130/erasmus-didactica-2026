# ErasmusDidactica 2026

**Erasmus+ KA1 — Teacher Training Mobility for Secondary Schools**

## Overview

ErasmusDidactica is a KA1 staff mobility project under the Erasmus+ programme (2026–2028). The project supports professional development of secondary school teachers through structured training courses, job shadowing, and teaching assignments across four partner countries.

## Partner Countries

| Country | Focus Area | Language |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| 🇩🇪 Germany | STEM methodology & dual education system | DE/EN |
| 🇫🇷 France | Inclusive education & differentiated pedagogy | FR/EN |
| 🇵🇱 Poland | Digital competences & e-learning platforms | PL/EN |
| 🇪🇸 Spain | Project-based learning & intercultural competences | ES/EN |

## Key Activities

1. **Structured Training Courses** — 5-day intensive courses hosted by each partner
2. **Job Shadowing** — 2-week observation periods in partner schools
3. **Teaching Assignments** — Short-term teaching exchanges (1–2 weeks)
4. **Transnational Project Meetings (TPM)** — Coordination meetings (2/year)

## Target Groups

- Secondary school teachers (ISCED 2–3)
- School administrators and coordinators
- 60+ participants over project lifetime
